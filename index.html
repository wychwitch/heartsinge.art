About Me

Lou Brown is a non-binary, midwestern game developer with specializations as a 3D generalist and as a 2D visual development artist. Lou is an energetic and fun faucet of creativity and has a strong conviction to bring unique and dazzling experiences to life, and create interactions that stay with people after the experience is over. Currently, they are a student at UW-STOUT. Working on their BFA in Game Design Development - Art.

They’re currently looking for work within a studio or company space, either as an intern or full-time employee.

They are proficient in Clip Studio Paint, Maya, Substance Painter, Unity, Unreal, and ZBrush!
